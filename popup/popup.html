To report potentially false or misleading content using World Wide Check, follow these steps:

  1. Install the World Wide Check Chrome extension by following the installation instructions provided.
  2. While browsing a webpage, look for any content that you believe may be misinformation.
  3. Click on the World Wide Check extension icon in your Chrome browser toolbar.
  4. A popup window will appear, allowing you to provide details about the suspicious content you want to report.
  5. Fill in the necessary information, including the URL of the webpage and a description of the misleading information.
  6. Click the "Submit" or "Report" button to send the report.
  7. Your report will be submitted and added to the collective effort in combating misinformation.

By reporting potentially false or misleading content, you are contributing to a safer online environment and helping to ensure the accuracy of information on the internet.

World Wide Check

World Wide Check is a powerful Chrome extension designed to combat misinformation on the internet. It enables users to report and flag potentially false or misleading content directly from webpages, contributing to a safer online environment.

How it Works

World Wide Check leverages a vast network of internet users and the collective wisdom of the online community to identify and report misleading information. By utilizing a subreddit dedicated to World Wide Check (r/worldwidecheck), all reported content is carefully examined and verified by a community of vigilant users.

Please visit r/worldwidecheck to join the community and contribute to the fight against misinformation.

Key Features

  • Report Misinformation: With World Wide Check, you can easily report suspicious content found on webpages, helping to prevent the spread of misinformation.
  • Community-driven Verification: The reported content is analyzed by the vigilant community on the dedicated subreddit, ensuring accurate and reliable identification of false information.
  • Chrome Extension: World Wide Check is a Chrome extension, providing a seamless experience for users, enabling them to report and flag content directly from their browser.

Installation

To install the World Wide Check Chrome extension, follow these steps:

  1. Download the extension package from the GitHub repository.
  2. Open your Chrome browser and go to chrome://extensions.
  3. Enable "Developer mode" in the top right corner of the page.
  4. Click on "Load unpacked" and select the downloaded extension package directory.
  5. The World Wide Check extension will be added to your browser.
